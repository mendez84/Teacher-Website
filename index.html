// about mr. mendez

Math teacher. Problem solver. Real one.

I teach math because it's one of the most hated subjects in the world — and it isn't as complicated as everyone makes it seem. The problem isn't the math. It's the story people tell themselves about it.

I've spent over 8 years in high school math classrooms at Palm Springs Unified, and before that another 4 years as a district-level Instructional Technology specialist helping teachers use technology more effectively. That background shapes how I teach — I build lessons that connect, use tools that actually help, and create an environment where it's okay not to know yet.

The win I chase isn't a test score. It's the moment when a student who's been telling themselves they "just can't do math" starts asking better questions. That shift in thinking — that's the whole point.

My classroom is chill. You'll see pieces of what I'm into — music, video game soundtracks, anime, games. But don't mistake the vibe for a lack of direction. We show up, we work, and we figure it out together.
